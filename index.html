general questions

Are there monogamous animals?

Yes, the seahorse and penguin are good examples of them.

do animales change sex?

Yes, all clownfish (Nemo), for example, are born male and will only change sex to become a dominant female.

Is homosexuality and bisexuality present in animals?

Yes, it is. Some of these include: birds, beetles, sheep, fruit bats, dolphins, orangutans, penguins, ostrichs, flamingos, monkeys, etc.
